## About me

I'm a Software Engineer from **Curitiba, Brazil**, currently working as a **Fullstack Software Engineer at ztrax**.

Today, my work is focused on **backend engineering, microservices, reliability, observability and production debugging**. I enjoy solving real-world engineering problems: slow pipelines, duplicated processing, distributed locks, service boundaries, queues, legacy modernization and systems that need to keep running under pressure.

I have been working with **Go, gRPC/Protobuf, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, Docker and AWS**, while also keeping a strong fullstack background with **TypeScript, React, Next.js and Angular**.

Outside of code, I'm the father of **Miguel and Manu**, a proud nerd, deeply into **games and RPGs**, passionate about **music**, and currently exploring my creative side as a future **beatmaker / music producer**.

I'm also passionate about **cars and mechanics** — programmable fuel injection, getting my hands dirty, learning how things work under the hood and enjoying the beauty of real-world engineering.

Technology is my main craft, but **creativity, machines and curiosity** are what keep me moving.
